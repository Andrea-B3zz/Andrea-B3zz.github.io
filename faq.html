Frequently Asked Question:

These are the most asked question, anyway you can access this site for every other doubt

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
    You have to go to this Link and click on "I want access to an office printer".
  • How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
    You can access the printer as said before and select scan and the document
  • How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
    You have to go to this site and then you can buy whatever you want.
  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
    You have to go to this Link and click on "Reserve room".
  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
    You have to present your pass at the barrier or ring the bell.
